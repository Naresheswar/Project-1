CSK

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is a professional franchise cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. They compete in the Indian Premier League (IPL), the top tier of Indian T20 cricket. CSK is one of the most successful teams in IPL history, having won multiple IPL titles and consistently qualifying for the playoffs.

CSK was founded in 2008 and is led for most of its history by MS Dhoni. The team is known for its strong fan base, consistent performance, and experienced core squad. CSK holds the record for most playoff appearances and is widely regarded as one of the most successful franchises in cricket league history.
