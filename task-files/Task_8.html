Chennai Super Kings

About Us

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is a professional cricket franchise based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The team competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and is one of the most successful teams in IPL history.

Achievements

CSK has won multiple IPL titles and is known for its consistency and strong team spirit.

Captain

MS Dhoni, fondly called “Thala”, is the face of CSK and one of the greatest captains in cricket.

Feature

CSK is famous for its yellow jersey, loyal fan base, and the iconic slogan “Whistle Podu”.
