Club Heritage:

Chennai Super Kings' heritage began in 2008 as one of the founding teams of the Indian Premier League (IPL). Known as the "Men in Yellow", CSK is famous for its consistency, strong leadership under MS Dhoni, passionate fanbase called the "Yellow Army", home dominance at MA Chidambaram Stadium, and multiple IPL titles, making it one of the most successful franchises in IPL history.
